About Mini Apple Music

"How was the show?" - this is the question that inspired this site. Mini Apple Music is a Twin Cities live music blog. Minneapolis has a thriving Music scene and I wanted to create a place to chat, gossip and review local shows. All views and opinions presented on Mini Apple Music are solely those the author.
